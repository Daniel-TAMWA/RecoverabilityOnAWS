Minimum RTO for a single AZ outage : 10 minutes 
Desciption
After the failure has occurred if we are in a 
zone of availability it will take some time for the zone of availability to change.

Minimum RTO for a single region outage: 60 minutes
Desciption
After the fault has occurred, the company's management must identify the problem
 and then begin repairs until the system is back in service.

Minimum RPO for a single AZ outage: 1 minute
Desciption
RPO or Recovery Point Objective, refers to the maximum amount of time it is acceptable to lose data during a failure.

In our case we are dealing with AWS databases, so less than a minute.

Minimum RPO for a single region outage: 5 minutes
Desciption
In this case it will take some time to switch to another region so it will take a few minutes.
